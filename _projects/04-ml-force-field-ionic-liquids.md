### Project description

Built a **graph neural network-based machine-learned force field** for complex ionic liquids, using **active learning sampling** and **ensemble uncertainty** to robustly expand training data.

### What it enabled

- Molecular dynamics of complex ionic-liquid systems up to **mesoscale million-atom simulations** (work listed as manuscript under preparation on my resume).

### Technologies

- GNN potentials
- Active learning loops for data efficiency
- Large-scale MD execution and analysis (LAMMPS-style workflows)
