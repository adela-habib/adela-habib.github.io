### Overview

Developed and tested a **real-time first-principles density-matrix dynamics framework** within **relativistic DFT** to study **spin relaxation and ultrafast spin dynamics** in materials such as **graphene**.

### Publications connected to this direction

- *Electric field and substrate dramatically accelerate spin relaxation in graphene* (Phys. Rev. B, 2022)
- *Spin-phonon relaxation from a universal ab initio density matrix approach* (Nat. Commun., 2020)
- *Ab initio ultrafast spin dynamics in solids* (Phys. Rev. B, 2021)

### Technologies

- MPI parallelization
- CUDA acceleration
- C++ scientific software development
