About

I’m a research scientist working at the intersection of computational physics/chemistry and machine learning. My work centers on building accurate and scalable simulation and ML pipelines—from first-principles electronic structure to data-driven interatomic potentials and agentic workflows that automate scientific tasks.

I’m especially interested in accelerated scientific computing on modern hardware (GPUs / mixed precision) and in physics-informed ML that can provide orders-of-magnitude speedups while preserving interpretability.
