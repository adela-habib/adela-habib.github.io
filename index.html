Featured Work

Research Projects

Protein Design · AI

Geometric Deep Learning for Protein Interaction

Developed a binding-site prediction algorithm coupling coarse surface geometry graphs with geometric transformers and protein LLMs — achieving 6× speedup and ~8% accuracy improvement over state-of-the-art models for antibody–antigen identification.

Manuscript in Prep.
Electron Dynamics · ML

HIPPYNN: ML Models for Plasmon Dynamics

Physics-informed GNN time-series model (PyTorch) predicting electron trajectories in silver nanoparticles — achieving a 10,000× speedup over rt-TDDFT methods. Published in J. Phys. Chem. A.

⌥ GitHub ↗ Paper
Force Fields · MD

ML Force Field for Ionic Liquids

Robust graph-neural-network ML force field built with active learning and ensemble uncertainty for million-atom MD simulations of complex ionic liquids using LAMMPS.

Manuscript in Prep.
Agentic AI · Materials

URSA: AI Agentic Workflow for Materials Discovery

Flexible multi-agent system closing the materials discovery loop from literature search through DFT/MD simulations to outcome review using reasoning mechanisms and scientific tooling (LAMMPS, ASE).

⌥ GitHub Manuscript in Prep.
HPC · Quantum

InvFactorize: GPU-Optimized Matrix Solver

Iterative matrix inverse solver on Nvidia Tensor cores (C++/CUDA) yielding a 3× speedup over conventional solvers for scalable quantum molecular dynamics. Published in J. Chem. Theory Comput.

↗ Paper
Spintronics · DFT

FeynWann: Real-Time Spin Dynamics Framework

First-principles density matrix dynamics within relativistic DFT (MPI, CUDA, C++) for studying spin relaxation in graphene and MoS₂. Published in Phys. Rev. B and Nature Communications.
